TEST 1: One-Way ANOVA - Revenue across Sales Channels
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
H0: Mean revenue is equal across all sales channels (Mobile App, Online, In-Store)
H1: At least one channel's mean revenue differs from the others

Group statistics:
  Mobile App     n=  379   mean=$    808.71   std=$    993.21
  Online         n= 1012   mean=$    778.99   std=$   1010.95
  In-Store       n=  609   mean=$    744.73   std=$    965.44

F-statistic        = 0.5079
p-value             = 0.601852
Levene's test p     = 0.4911  (homogeneity of variance: satisfied)
Eta-squared (effect size) = 0.0005

DECISION: FAIL TO REJECT H0 (p >= 0.05)
CONCLUSION: No statistically significant difference in mean revenue across sales
channels. Channel explains virtually none of the variance in order revenue (eta-sq ~ 0).
